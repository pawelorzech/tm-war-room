# TM Hub — Your Faction Toolkit

Hey Masters,

I've been building a tool for our faction and it's ready for you to check out: **hub.tri.ovh**

## What is it?

TM Hub is a web app designed specifically for The Masters. You log in with your Torn API key (it's stored encrypted, used only to pull your data from Torn API) and get access to a bunch of tools in one place.

## What's inside?

**Faction stuff:**
- **Dashboard** — faction overview at a glance
- **Our Team** — member list with stats and status
- **Enemies** — enemy faction intel
- **Activity** — who's online, who's idle
- **War Reports** — ranked war history and breakdowns
- **OC Planner** — organized crime coordination
- **Chain Tracker** — live chain monitoring
- **Analytics** — faction performance trends

**Combat & targets:**
- **Targets** — find targets that match your stats
- **Bounties** — current bounty board
- **Spy Central** — stat estimates for players
- **Compare** — compare yourself against other players
- **Stakeout** — watch specific players for status changes
- **NPC Loot** — NPC loot timer tracker
- **Revives** — revive requests and history

**Economy & tools:**
- **Market** — item bazaar price checker
- **Stocks** — stock portfolio and market data
- **Travel** — item prices abroad and profit calculator
- **Companies** — company specials and info

**Learning:**
- **Training Guide** — stat training strategy and calculators
- **Stat Growth** — track your stat progression over time
- **Awards** — honor bar tracker — see what you're close to earning
- **FAQ** — game mechanics explained

## Heads up

This is still a work in progress. Some pages might show incomplete data, some calculations might be off, and not everything has been battle-tested with real scenarios yet. **I'd really appreciate your feedback** — what works, what's broken, what's confusing, what you'd want added.

If something looks wrong or a page isn't loading properly, let me know. That's the whole point of sharing it now — I want to fix things before calling it "done."

## How to get started

1. Go to **hub.tri.ovh**
2. Enter your Torn API key (limited access key is fine)
3. Explore and let me know what you think
